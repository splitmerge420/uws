### Key Implementation Details

- **Independence by Default:** The `ProviderMigration` and `ZeroKnowledgeIdentity` components are designed to give the user the ultimate freedom — the ability to leave any provider at any time without losing their digital life, and the ability to prove their identity without revealing their master credentials.

- **Agent Safety and Control:** The `AgenticPause` and `UniversalUndo` features provide critical safety mechanisms for autonomous agent operation, preventing burnout and allowing for easy rollback of unintended actions.

- **Extensibility and Composability:** The `SkillsMarketplace` and `HotSwapReasoning` components make the system infinitely extensible. New capabilities can be added by dropping in a `SKILL.md` file, and complex tasks can be solved by composing different agents in a single reasoning chain.

- **Conflict as a Feature:** The `ConflictResolution` protocol treats disagreement between agents not as an error, but as a feature. It provides a structured way to surface different perspectives and escalate to the Sovereign for a final decision, making the system more robust and transparent.

This layer delivers on the promise of agentic sovereignty, ensuring that the user is always in control, their data is always their own, and their agents are always working for them.
