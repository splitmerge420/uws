# Blackout Simulation: Day 16 — The Orphan Node

**Event:** Northern node loses all connectivity

## Status

A northern satrapy node loses all connectivity — HF, mesh, and satellite. Complete isolation.

## System Response

1. Neighboring Node-Beta detects orphan status
2. Node-Beta projects a "thin client" into the orphan sector via mesh
3. Shared Burden Tax applied: vouchers in Node-Beta reduced by 4%
4. Audit continues unbroken through the projected thin client

## Attempted Exploit

Local government attempts to issue paper rations during the disconnection. The Lattice broadcasts (via remaining mesh) that digital credits remain valid and paper rations will incur a 200% penalty upon resync.

Result: Citizens reject paper rations. The protocol's credibility holds even during total node isolation.

## Shared Burden Coefficient Applied

V_final = V_base x (1 - P_orphan/P_total x sigma)

Node-Beta absorbed a 4% overhead — a small tax to keep the audit alive.
