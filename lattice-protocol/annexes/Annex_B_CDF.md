# Annex B — Collective Defense Fund (CDF)

## Funding

- 2% of every barrel loaded and 2% of every infrastructure milestone payment.

## Liability

- Repairs covered immediately within 48 hours (no-fault insurance).
- If TDP identifies an aggressor (>90% certainty), the aggressor is billed 300% of repair cost, deducted from future revenue.

## No-Fault Principle

Removes the "attribution trap" — infrastructure heals itself, political debates happen after. This ensures kinetic damage never disrupts the debt-retirement waterfall or humanitarian distributions.
