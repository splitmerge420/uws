# Annex E — Glass-Pocket Protocol (Representative Integrity)

## Disclosure

All board and TDP members must file full financial disclosure on the immutable ledger. No exceptions.

## Slashing Formula

Any undisclosed foreign influence triggers an automatic penalty:

P_penalty = 0.10 x T_current

Where T_current is the stakeholder's share of the current debt-retirement tranche. The penalty is deducted directly from the stakeholder's balance sheet.

## Automation (Phase III)

Phase III (Months 18+) migrates to API-linked monitoring — penalty executes automatically when bank data hits the ledger. No human discretion required.
