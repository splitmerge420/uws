# Annex C — Dispute Resolution & Grey State

## 14-Day Grace Period

A >10% variance in any monitored metric triggers a "Grey State." Normalization clocks are paused, not reset. This prevents minor anomalies from destroying months of progress.

## Margin Freeze

If a Material Breach (fraud or kinetic strike) is certified by the TDP, all $120B+ in deployed capital is locked until compliance is restored. No party can withdraw or redirect funds during a Margin Freeze.

## Technical Dispute Panel (TDP)

- 9 members (3 US, 3 China, 3 Iran)
- 6/9 supermajority required for any "Certificate of Material Breach"
- Ratified by 2/3 majority of the Lattice Node Network (12 rotating neutral nations)
