# Annex D — Verification Standards (Telemetric Truth)

## Hydrocarbon Verification

- Synthetic Aperture Radar (SAR) satellite monitors volumetric tanker draft
- Automatic Identification System (AIS) tracking
- TEE-signed digital bills of lading at every pump station
- Physical tampering wipes cryptographic keys

## Grid Verification

- Thermal satellite imagery for energy output verification
- IoT edge nodes (TEE-sealed) reporting megawatt output in real-time
- Cross-referenced against expected capacity

## Biometric Verification (COLP Distribution)

- Zero-Knowledge Proofs (ZKPs) verify Cost of Living distributions without exposing personal data
- Monthly "48-Hour Blitz" — random sample of 10,000 COLP recipients must pass a liveness & logic check
- Integrity calculated on a 90-day moving average
- >95% biometric integrity required for Tier 1 normalization milestone
