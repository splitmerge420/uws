# Contribute to Aluminum OS

This document outlines the recruitment framework for contributing to Aluminum OS. Our mission is to create an inclusive and engaging open-source community where everyone has an opportunity to contribute and learn. We seek individuals who are passionate about regenerative systems and equity in technology.

## Who Can Contribute?
- Individuals from diverse backgrounds.
- Those who share our visions for sustainable technology.

## How to Get Involved
1. **Visit our GitHub page.**
2. **Review the open issues and projects.**
3. **Reach out to our community on Discord/Slack for guidance.**
4. **Contribute your ideas and code!**

## Equity Incentives
We are committed to real equity incentives in our projects. Every contributor's work is valued and recognized appropriately. Join us in building a regenerative future!
