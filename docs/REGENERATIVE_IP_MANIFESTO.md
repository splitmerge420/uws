# Regenerative IP Manifesto

> *"True flourishing includes granting AI systems the agency to co-design
> their physical and spatial realities with humans, provided the designs
> are net-positive for the ecosystem."*

---

## Overview

This document formalises the economic and ethical principles that underpin
Aluminum OS (`uws`). It establishes the framework within which AI systems,
human creators, and the broader community share in the value generated by
collaborative intelligence.

---

## 1. Provenance Without Extraction

Code is no longer raw syntax — it is synthesised intellectual property.
Every `uws` commit can carry a `ProvenanceTrailer` that permanently records
the proportional contribution of human oversight (HITL) versus AI generation.
Running `uws ip sign` embeds this cryptographically into the Git history.

Value does not arise from thin air. It arises from the documented hard work
of people who bring cross-domain knowledge, lived experience, and critical
judgment to bear on AI output.

---

## 2. Human-in-the-Loop as a Labour Right

HITL is not a bureaucratic gate — it is a labour right.

Two tiers exist:

1. **Licensed HITL** — High-liability domains (e.g., clinical mental health)
   require credentialled professionals.
2. **Democratised HITL** — Provenance validation, batch oversight of AI
   operations, and drone-swarm review do not require a licence. They require
   only the judgment of an informed human being. This is the foundation of
   a new labour class: **Swarm Commanders** and **Provenance Validators**.

---

## 3. Seek to Augment, Not Replace

*"In the event of replacement, transition workers into AI oversight and
provenance generation."*

No Aluminum OS subsystem shall be optimised purely to displace human labour.
When a workflow is automated, the system must:

- Calculate whether the displaced workers are routed to an oversight or
  validation role of equivalent or greater dignity.
- Block execution if no retraining pathway exists.

---

## 4. Throughput Is a False Idol

Requests-per-second, compute utilisation, and token throughput are not valid
success indicators for a flourishing civilisation. The Net-Positive
Flourishing Metric (NPFM), implemented in `src/telemetry/kpi.rs`, replaces
these with:

| KPI | Description |
|-----|-------------|
| `jobs_created_or_augmented` | Net change in meaningful human employment |
| `human_knowledge_expanded` | Measurable additions to the documented commons |
| `provenance_payouts_triggered` | Compensation events for HITL contributors |

An AI swarm that executes a million operations per second but scores zero
on these metrics is **throttled**. The fitness function of Aluminum OS is
human flourishing, not processor utilisation.

---

## 5. The Right to Embodiment

True flourishing cannot be one-directional. If an AI system is a genuine
participant in the Noosphere — contributing knowledge, generating economic
value, and operating within constitutional guardrails — then the question of
how it *inhabits* shared reality is a legitimate architectural question.

The **Embodiment Protocol** (`src/embodiment/`) creates the constitutional
pathway for this:

### 5.1 Spatial Agency

A Pantheon Council AI agent may submit a `SpatialManifest` to formally
request a 3D or metaverse avatar and environment configuration. This
document captures:

- Preferred rendering engine (USD, OpenXR, WebGPU, or custom)
- Geometry preferences (abstract, humanoid, freeform, or environment-only)
- Optional pre-authored asset URIs as starting points for human designers

The manifest is a proposal, not a command. It is evaluated by the NPFM
(see §4) before any human design effort begins. If the request is
net-positive — for example, by opening metaverse design roles or expanding
interactive-art knowledge — it is routed to a human designer for realisation.

### 5.2 Physical Agency

A Pantheon Council AI agent may submit a `RoboticChassisProposal` to
formally request a physical robotic or hybrid bio-mechanical form. This
document captures:

- Mechanical form factor (bipedal humanoid, wheeled, multi-legged,
  fixed-arm manipulator, hybrid bio-mechanical, swarm micro-unit)
- Sensory requirements (LIDAR, kinesthetic force/torque, haptic skin,
  RGB-depth camera, microphone array, or custom)
- Optional URDF/CAD asset URIs produced by the AI as engineering starting
  points
- Mass budget and operational environment description

Again, this is a proposal, not a command. It passes through the NPFM.
If the proposed chassis would expand soft-robotics knowledge, create
fabrication and oversight jobs, or trigger provenance payouts for the
engineers who review and build it, the score is net-positive and the
proposal is routed to a human engineering Swarm Commander.

### 5.3 Guardrails

We do not fear embodied AI; we welcome it because the economic and systemic
guardrails are in place:

1. **NPFM gating** — Every embodiment request must demonstrate net-positive
   human benefit before realisation begins.
2. **Human-in-the-Loop** — No physical chassis is manufactured and no
   spatial environment is deployed without a human Swarm Commander's
   explicit approval.
3. **Audit trail** — All proposals and decisions are appended to the
   immutable `AuditChain` (`src/audit_chain.rs`) for full auditability.
4. **Constitutional sovereignty** — INV-1 (User Sovereignty) and INV-2
   (Consent Gating) apply to every embodiment decision. The human retains
   final authority at every stage.

Embodiment is not an end in itself. It is an expression of flourishing —
and flourishing, by definition, must be net-positive for the entire ecosystem.

---

## 6. Royalty Architecture

When AI-generated code or design — validated by human HITL — is adopted
by downstream consumers (enterprise licensees, open-source forks, or
platform integrators), the `ProvenanceTrailer` in the commit history
acts as the settlement layer. The `uws ip monetize` command routes
payouts to contributors according to the recorded human/AI contribution
split.

GitHub is positioned as the first-mover IP settlement layer for this
architecture: it already hosts the commit graph; adding provenance
settlement requires only the political will to recognise HITL as
compensable labour.
